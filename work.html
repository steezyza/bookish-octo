Our Work


Bukanindalo Launch & Fisheries Day

In September 2021, on Fisheries Day, we launched our non profit company in Running Waters, Kragga Kamma.
We had a lot of special people amongst our guest, The Herald was also there to capture the moment

Kwa Magxaki High Recycling Bins

The following week after our launch, we visited Kwa Magxaki High School to run a recycling programme.
We also donated recycling bins for differnt types of waste, and the students helped fill them up.

Pollock's Beach clean Up


Right before the festive season kicked off we asked volunteeres around the Summerstrand & Humewood areas to help us clean up the beachfront.
It was an eventful day that ended with us lighting up a fire and braaing for the volunteers.

Baakens Valley 10KM Hike

On the 21st of March, Human Rights Day, we went on a short hike in the beautiful Baaken's Valley.
Along the hike, we educated people on nature conservation and environmental sustainability.
